About SkillScribe.

Be the STAR of your experience.

SkillScribe is a new work experience management web application that provide you with a fast and effective way to record and review day to day work experience following the S.T.A.R format, which encourages you to highlight the Situation, Task, Action and Result of your experience. To assist you further, each personalised entry is based on one of the many currently available and ever-growing list of behavioural interview questions you may encounter in an interview. This helps towards curating a personalised database of experience that can be effectively communicated in an interview setting with little need for prior revision.
